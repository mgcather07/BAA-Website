Our Mission

Preserve. Protect. Educate.

The BAA was incorporated in 1989 to manage bowfishing tournaments across the United States. Today our mission is broader: to preserve, protect, and provide education on the sport of bowfishing — working alongside biologists and natural-resource departments to control invasive and non-game fish populations.
