Why It Matters

There is strength in numbers

The sport of bowfishing is constantly under attack — from anti-hunting groups and animal-rights activists to carp and gar angler groups. Any organization is only as strong as its member base, and the Bowfishing Association of America is no exception.

"There is strength in numbers" has never been more true than it is today. Your membership directly strengthens our advocacy and helps protect the rights of bowfishermen everywhere.
