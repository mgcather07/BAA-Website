A short history

The Bowfishing Association of America was incorporated in 1989 to organize and manage bowfishing tournaments across the United States, and has proudly carried the banner of the sport since 1990.

Over three-plus decades, the BAA has grown from a tournament body into a national non-profit advocate — defending access, educating the public, and partnering with conservation agencies. The World Championship remains the crown jewel of the competitive calendar, crowning the best teams in the country year after year.
