Tournament Sanctioning

Make your event count — get it recognized under the BAA banner.

For Tournament Directors

Why sanction with the BAA?

BAA sanctioning connects your local or regional tournament to a national community. Sanctioned results can contribute Bowfisher of the Year points for participating members, giving your shooters something bigger to compete for.

Sanctioning lends your event credibility, consistent standards, and visibility across the BAA's nationwide membership — whether you're running a single charity shoot or a full series.

How it works

  1. Submit a sanctioning request with your event details and date.
  2. Our team reviews it and confirms requirements with you.
  3. Once approved, your event is recognized and eligible to award BOTY points to BAA members.
