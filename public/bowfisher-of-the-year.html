Bowfisher of the Year

A season-long race for bragging rights as the nation's top bowfisher.

The Contest

Points all season long

The Bowfisher of the Year (BOTY) contest rewards consistency across the whole season. Points are earned from BAA-sanctioned tournaments and from submitted fish records — so every event and every giant you stick can move you up the standings.

BOTY points are a Platinum membership benefit. Platinum members accumulate points from sanctioned tournaments and fish records throughout the year.

How To Earn Points

Two paths to the top

Sanctioned tournaments

Compete in BAA-sanctioned events around the country. Your finishes translate into BOTY points toward the season total.

Fish records

Submit qualifying catches as fish records. Records contribute points too — rewarding the anglers chasing giants year-round.
